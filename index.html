SRSD Writing Tutor

A guided continuation-writing website for students who need structured, patient, step-by-step support.

The tutor follows an SRSD teaching sequence: key information extraction, story mountain planning, sentence-by-sentence writing, and revision. Students log in with their name and student ID, then complete tasks under article type A or B.

Student Login How It Works
For Students

One small step at a time

Students are not given a full essay. They are guided to think, answer, write, and revise actively.

For Records

Tracked by identity and article type

Each tutoring record is linked to student name, student ID, and article type A or B.

Stage 1

Key Information Extraction

Identify characters, setting, conflict, emotions, clues, and theme.

Stage 2

Story Mountain Planning

Build a clear continuation path for Paragraph 1 and Paragraph 2.

Stage 3

Sentence-by-Sentence Writing

Write with frames, words, and teacher prompts instead of copying a model essay.

Stage 4

Feedback and Revision

Improve logic, language, consistency, and theme after drafting.

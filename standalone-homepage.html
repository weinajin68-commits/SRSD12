SRSD Writing Tutor

A continuation-writing website that guides students one small step at a time.

This site follows the SRSD teaching structure: key information extraction, story mountain planning, sentence-by-sentence writing, and final revision. Students enter their name, student ID, and article type A or B before starting a tutoring session.

Stage 1: Extract Key Information

Students identify characters, setting, conflict, emotions, plot clues, and theme before writing.

Stage 2: Plan the Story Mountain

Students map Paragraph 1 and Paragraph 2 in a logical continuation path.

Stage 3: Write Sentence by Sentence

The tutor gives short prompts, frames, and vocabulary instead of a full model essay.

Stage 4: Revise the Draft

Students improve plot logic, language accuracy, character consistency, and theme.
